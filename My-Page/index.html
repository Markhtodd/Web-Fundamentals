Reps and sets

Reps are 12 to 18 except abdominal exercises are 25 reps.

Do 2 exercises back to back for 2 to 3 sets without rest on chest and back as well as legs.

For arms and shoulders do 3 exercises (deltoids, biceps, triceps) one after the other for 2 sets each. No rest.

This is just an example. Consult your doctor before staring any training.
