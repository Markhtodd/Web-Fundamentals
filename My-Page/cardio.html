This page gives cardio zone training examples to help you get the most out of your cardio in the least amount of time!

Zone training

If you do not know your max heart rate minus your age from 220.

All percentages are based on this number.

This is just an example. Consult your doctor before training.


Low Intensity/Recovery Day

Just Walk

Moderate Intensity

Step-1 Warm-up 5 Min

Step-2 Increase Heart Rate to Zone 2 (75-85% of max) for One Minute

Step-3 Decrease to Zone One (65-75% of max) for Two Minutes

Repeat Steps 2-3 for the Desired Time

Finish 5 Min Cool Down

High Intensity

Step-1 Warm-up 5 Min

Step-2 Increase Heart Rate to (75-85% of max) for Two Minutes

Step-3 Increase Heart Rate to Zone 3 (86-95% of max) for One Minute

Step-4 Decrease Heart Rate to Zone 2 (75-85% of max) for One Minute

Step5 Increase Heart Rate to Zone 3 (86-95% of max) for One Minute

Repeat Steps 2-5 for the Desired Time

Finish 5 Min Cool Down
